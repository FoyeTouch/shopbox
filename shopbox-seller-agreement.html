1. Partnership Overview
This agreement is between you ("Seller") and Shopbox Nigeria ("Platform"). By signing this agreement, you become an approved Shopbox Partner Seller. Shopbox is a marketplace platform only — we do not own, manufacture, or warehouse your products. You remain fully responsible for the authenticity, quality, and accurate description of every product you list.
2. Commission & Payments
Shopbox earns a commission on every sale (see rates above)
Customer payments go directly to Shopbox's account
Your earnings are held for 14 days after delivery confirmation
Payouts processed weekly every Friday to your registered bank account
First payout requires minimum 30 days on platform
Minimum payout amount: ₦5,000
Shopbox reserves the right to withhold payment during dispute resolution
3. Product Listing Rules
Maximum 5 products per day upload limit
Maximum 5 photos per product (real photos only — no stock images)
All products are reviewed by Shopbox admin before going live
Product description must be accurate, complete and honest
Price must reflect actual market value — suspicious pricing will be flagged
Deletion requests require admin approval before product is removed
Out-of-stock updates are immediate but subject to admin review
Shopbox may edit, remove, or suspend any listing without prior notice
4. Product Authenticity
You declare that all products are genuine, authentic, and legally obtained. Selling counterfeit, stolen, or prohibited goods will result in immediate permanent account termination, reporting to Nigerian authorities (FCCPC), and legal action where applicable. Shopbox reserves the right to request proof of authenticity at any time.
5. Delivery Responsibility
You are responsible for your own delivery logistics
You must specify accurate delivery timeframes on each product
Orders must be confirmed within 24 hours of placement
Failure to confirm within 24 hours = automatic order cancellation
Repeated late confirmations = account suspension
Products must match photos and description exactly
6. Returns & Refunds
Buyer has 5 days from delivery to request return
Returns only accepted for wrong item, damaged, or not as described
If return approved due to seller fault — seller bears return shipping cost
Refund deducted from seller's pending earnings
Shopbox's decision on all disputes is final and binding
7. Strike System — Zero Tolerance
Strike 1: Warning + 7-day product review suspension
Strike 2: 30-day account suspension + earnings held
Strike 3: Permanent ban + legal referral if applicable
Instant ban offences: fake products, scam activity, threatening buyers, false information

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Disputes shall first be resolved through Shopbox mediation. Shopbox's decision is final. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to appropriate Nigerian courts. By signing, you confirm you are at least 18 years of age and legally authorised to conduct business.
9. Shopbox Rights
Shopbox reserves the right to: modify commission rates (30 days notice), remove any listing, suspend or terminate any seller account, withhold payments during investigations, update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of new terms.
